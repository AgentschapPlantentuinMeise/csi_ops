# SOP 02 — Calibration Standards and Internal Standard Solutions

## 1. Purpose
To describe the preparation of:
- Multi-element calibration standards from **Periodic Element Mix 1**  
- Individual **K** and **Ca** calibration standards  
- **Yttrium (Y)** internal standard stock and calibration standards  

All solutions are prepared in a **5% NO₃ matrix** for ICP-OES analysis.

## 2. Scope
Applicable to all ICP-OES calibration and internal standard preparations in the CSI laboratory.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Analysts**: Prepare standards according to this SOP and document all batches.
- **Lab coordinator**: Verify consistency and traceability.
- **Quality officer**: Review calibration and QC records.

## 4. Safety
- All solutions contain nitric acid; follow acid handling procedures.
- Refer to SOP 01 (5% NO₃ stock) and CSI hazard sheets.

---

## 5. Materials and Reagents

- **Periodic Element Mix 1**  
  - 33 elements at **10 ppm** in **10% NO₃**
- **K stock solution**: 1000 ppm in nitric acid
- **Ca stock solution**: 1000 ppm in nitric acid
- **Y stock solution**: 1000 ppm in nitric acid
- **5% NO₃ stock solution** (from SOP 01)
- Milli-Q water
- Volumetric flasks (10, 20, 40, 50, 100, 1000 mL)
- Acid-compatible pipettes and tips
- HDPE or glass bottles for storage

## 6. Multi-element calibration standards from Periodic Element Mix 1

### 6.1 Preparation of 5 ppm intermediate in 5% NO₃

Starting solution: **Periodic Element Mix 1**, 10 ppm in 10% NO₃.

1. Pipette **20 mL** of Periodic Element Mix 1 into a **40 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **Milli-Q water**.
3. Result:
   - Element concentration: **5 ppm**
   - Matrix: **5% NO₃** (10% → 5% by 1:2 dilution)

### 6.2 Preparation of 1 ppm intermediate in 5% NO₃

1. Pipette **10 mL** of the **5 ppm** intermediate into a **50 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **5% NO₃ stock** (SOP 01).
3. Result:
   - Element concentration: **1 ppm**
   - Matrix: **5% NO₃**

### 6.3 Preparation of 0.5 ppm intermediate in 5% NO₃

1. Pipette **20 mL** of the **1 ppm** intermediate into a **40 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **5% NO₃ stock**.
3. Result:
   - Element concentration: **0.5 ppm**
   - Matrix: **5% NO₃**

> **Typical calibration series** (multi-element):  
> 0 (blank, 5% NO₃), 0.5 ppm, 1 ppm, 5 ppm  
> Adjust as needed per method.

## 9. Yttrium (Y) internal standard stock and calibration standards

### 9.1 Preparation of 10 ppm Y internal standard stock (0.05% NO₃)

Starting solution: **1000 ppm Y**.

1. Pipette **10 mL** of 1000 ppm Y into a **1000 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **Milli-Q water**.
3. Result:
   - **10 ppm Y**
   - Matrix: approx. **0.05% NO₃** (from original acid)

This stock is used for:
- Spiking samples (e.g. **2 mL per digestion vessel**, as per digestion SOP).
- Preparing Y calibration standards.

### 9.2 Y calibration standards in 5% NO₃ (for internal standard channel)

Goal: Y standards in **5% NO₃** at concentrations suitable to calibrate/verify the internal standard response (e.g. 0.1–2 ppm).

Starting solution: **10 ppm Y in 0.05% NO₃** (Section 9.1) and **5% NO₃ stock** (SOP 01).

#### 9.2.1 2 ppm Y in 5% NO₃

1. Pipette **20 mL** of **10 ppm Y** into a **100 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **5% NO₃ stock**.
3. Result:
   - **2 ppm Y**
   - Matrix: ~5% NO₃.

#### 9.2.2 1 ppm Y in 5% NO₃

1. Pipette **10 mL** of **10 ppm Y** into a **100 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **5% NO₃ stock**.
3. Result:
   - **1 ppm Y**
   - Matrix: ~5% NO₃.

#### 9.2.3 0.5 ppm Y in 5% NO₃

1. Pipette **5 mL** of **10 ppm Y** into a **100 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **5% NO₃ stock**.
3. Result:
   - **0.5 ppm Y**
   - Matrix: ~5% NO₃.

#### 9.2.4 0.1 ppm Y in 5% NO₃ (optional low-level check)

1. Pipette **1 mL** of **10 ppm Y** into a **100 mL** volumetric flask.
2. Dilute to the mark with **5% NO₃ stock**.
3. Result:
   - **0.1 ppm Y**
   - Matrix: ~5% NO₃.

> **Suggested Y calibration/verification series**:  
> 0 (5% NO₃ blank), 0.1 ppm, 0.5 ppm, 1 ppm, 2 ppm Y in 5% NO₃.

These standards:
- Match the **5% NO₃ matrix** of the analyte standards.
- Span the typical internal standard concentration range used in ICP-OES.
- Allow verification that the internal standard channel responds linearly and is stable.

## 10. Labeling, Storage, and Stability

For all standards:
- Label with:
  - Analyte(s)
  - Concentration
  - Matrix (5% NO₃)
  - Batch ID
  - Date, preparer, expiry
- Store in acid-compatible bottles at room temperature, protected from light.
- Typical expiry:
  - Multi-element and Y standards: **1 month**
  - K and Ca standards: **1–3 months**, depending on stability studies.
