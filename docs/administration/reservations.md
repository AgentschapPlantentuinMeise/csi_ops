# Standard Operating Procedure – Resource Reservations

# 1\. Introduction

For smooth operations it is important to make timely reservations of required resources for the different Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
The procedure is always similar and therefore the different SOPs only mention the online resource location and refer to this SOP.

# 2\. Plan a reservation

A reservation must be planned into the labs shared google agenda to avoid conflicts.

1. Open Google Calendar on your device.  
2. Tap \<+ Create\> to create a reservation.  
3. Enter the title of the agenda-point that must consist of:  
   - WHO;  
   - WHERE (what lab-space): LOCATION;  
   - WHAT (experiment \+ possible need for fume-hood or appliances).  
     - List DEVICES
4. Enter the desired day and time-window.  
5. Select the appropriate room (LAB: LOCATION).  
6. Tap \<Save\> to save and create the reservation.

**The room and the apparatus must be reserved at least one day prior to the planned experiment\!**
